I’m Abid Hussain. I’ve spent more than fifteen years learning how a space should feel. Most of that time, I worked in 3D visualization. That means I built rooms before they were real. Thousands of them. I learned something you can’t learn any other way: what actually works, and what only looks good in a picture.

But I don’t think design is only about design. I think it’s about taste. And taste is one thing, not many. The same eye that knows a room is right is the eye that knows a dish is right. That’s why I cook. Why I care about food more than is reasonable. A good meal and a good home come from the same place.

So when you sit with me, I’m not going to hand you a trend. I’m going to read you. How you live, what you love, how you want to feel when you open your front door.
